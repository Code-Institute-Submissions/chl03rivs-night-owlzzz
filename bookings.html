Bookings

Although we accept walk-ins, we cannot ensure that there will always be enough room for your group on any given day. You can easily reserve a table or room by contacting our team using the form below, or by phoning: +353 833 221 100

Option 1: Free

A reserved area for a small group (not a private room)

  • Suits groups of 5 or less
  • Food and drinks not included
  • A €50 deposit required in advance

Option 2: €10 per person

A reserved area for larger groups (not a private room)

  • Suits groups of 10+
  • 1 free mocktail per person
  • €70 deposit required in advance

Option 3: Private room

Private rooms can be rented out for a day or longer periods of time.

  • Private rooms suit up to 30 people
  • Function room that can host up to 90 guests
  • The rental price will depend on group size and time/day of your booking
  • Deposit will be 20% of rental fee

Please note:

  • A deposit is required in order to secure your booking(s) and will be refunded on the night of.
  • The balance of your fees must be paid three days prior to your booking date at the latest.
