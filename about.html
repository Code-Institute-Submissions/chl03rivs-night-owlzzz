About

Who are we?

Night Owlzz is a family-owned business, born from the minds of brothers Billy and Kevin in the heart of Togher. What started as a simple drunken-night rant, turned into one of the greatest venues in the city.

"I'm never drinking again", said Kevin. "But where will we hang out then?", I retorted. Little did we know that so many of you felt the same way...

NightOwlzzz's mission is to represent a movement that breaks away from our "drinking culture".

We aim to show our local crowd that it is possible to have fun without alcohol and host a space for people to hang out until late without the common worries that come with regular night outs.

As a growing business, our inbox is always open for feedback, suggestions and offers. Feel free to send us an email at hello@nightowlzzz.com :)
