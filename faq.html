FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Not necessarily. We accept Walk-ins during our operating hours.

We cannot guarantee that there will be enough seats for your group on any given night, however, so we recommend bookings for larger groups (5+ members).

Can I bring my own game? Absolutely!
Do you accept donations?

Yes, we do accept donations! We ask that you do not bring your donations directly to our venue, as we cannot promise that your game will be accepted.

If you have a game/games you would like to donate, send us an email with the game(s)' details at donations@nightowlzzz.com.

Our team will review your offer and our needs and get back to you asap.

Are there easy-to-understand instructions for the games available?

Yes. All games come with laminated instructions.

Our friendly staff is also always available to help your party with game rules!

What games do you have?

Our game supply is always being updated. Have a look at our Games page for a list of all current games.

Can you host large groups?

The answer to that question depends on the group size. Head over to our Bookings page for a breakdown of our available options.

Once you fill out our booking inquiry form, our team will get back to you with our best offer for your needs.

Do you accept recurring reservations?

Yes! Head over to our Bookings page to fill out an inquiry form. Our team will review your request and get back to you with details of our availability.

If you didn't find an answer to your question(s), feel free to email us at hello@nightowlzzz.com with your concerns. Or pop into our shop and chat with one of our friendly staff!
